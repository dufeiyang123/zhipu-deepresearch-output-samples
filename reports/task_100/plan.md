# Section 1：章节研究计划

## Chapter 1：The New Middle Layer — How AI Mediates Human Communication

### 研究目标
- Establish the empirical landscape of AI-mediated communication in 2025–2026.
- Map the principal channels through which AI sits between human interlocutors: smart-compose, auto-reply, chatbot interfaces, AI-generated social-media content, real-time translation layers, and algorithmic content curation.
- Quantify the scale and penetration of these mediations across demographics and platforms.
- Key questions: (1) What proportion of everyday interpersonal communication now involves an AI intermediary? (2) Which groups are most/least exposed? (3) Does AI mediation systematically alter message character?

### 关键发现
- 发现 1: Hancock, Naaman, and Levy (2020) formally defined AI-Mediated Communication (AI-MC) as interpersonal communication in which an intelligent agent modifies, augments, or generates messages on behalf of a communicator. They identified five key dimensions: magnitude, media type, optimization goal, autonomy, and role orientation. [Hancock et al., JCMC 2020](https://academic.oup.com/jcmc/article/25/1/89/5714020 "AI-MC definition and research agenda, cited 815+ times")
- 发现 2: As of 2017, Google's Smart Reply accounted for 12% of all Gmail messages sent (~6.7 billion AI-written emails/day), establishing a baseline for AI penetration into interpersonal messaging. [Hohenstein et al., Scientific Reports 2023](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-023-30938-9 "Peer-reviewed study citing Google 2017 Smart Reply data")
- 发现 3: By July 2025, ChatGPT users collectively sent more than 2.5 billion messages per day (~29,000/sec); "seeking specific information" accounted for 18.3% of conversations and "writing" tasks declined from 36% to 24% of usage between July 2024 and July 2025. [DataReportal Digital 2026 Report](https://datareportal.com/reports/digital-2026-one-billion-people-using-ai "Citing NBER/OpenAI study data, October 2025")
- 发现 4: Over 1 billion people worldwide use standalone AI platforms monthly (excluding embedded AI). ChatGPT alone had ~750 million weekly active users by September 2025; China hosts ~250 million AI agent users. [DataReportal Digital 2026 Report](https://datareportal.com/reports/digital-2026-one-billion-people-using-ai "Kepios analysis of Similarweb data, October 2025")
- 发现 5: Google AI Overviews reach 2+ billion active users monthly; across six countries, 54% reported seeing an AI-generated search answer in the past week. [DataReportal Digital 2026 Report](https://datareportal.com/reports/digital-2026-one-billion-people-using-ai "October 2025")
- 发现 6: Across six countries, the share of people who have ever used standalone generative AI rose from 40% (2024) to 61% (2025); weekly use nearly doubled from 18% to 34% — roughly 3× faster than initial internet adoption in the late 1990s. [Reuters Institute Gen AI and News Report 2025](https://reutersinstitute.politics.ox.ac.uk/sites/default/files/2025-10/Gen_AI_and_News_Report_2025.pdf "YouGov survey of ~2,000 per country, June–July 2025")
- 发现 7: AI use skews by age: 59% of 18–24-year-olds used generative AI weekly vs. 20% among 55+; age differences are minimal for embedded AI tools (Copilot, Meta AI, Grok), indicating embedded mediation reaches demographics more uniformly. [Reuters Institute Gen AI and News Report 2025](https://reutersinstitute.politics.ox.ac.uk/sites/default/files/2025-10/Gen_AI_and_News_Report_2025.pdf "Figure 8, age breakdown")
- 发现 8: AI enthusiasm varies dramatically: 77% of Nigerian online adults are "excited" about AI vs. 33% in the US and below 30% in the UK. [DataReportal Digital 2026 Report](https://datareportal.com/reports/digital-2026-one-billion-people-using-ai "GWI data, October 2025")
- 发现 9: In randomized experiments (N=438 pairs + N=291 pairs), Hohenstein et al. (2023) found Google Smart Reply increased messaging speed by 10.2%, shifted language toward more positive sentiment, and led partners to evaluate each other as more cooperative and affiliative. However, when participants suspected AI use, they rated partners as significantly less cooperative (t(92)=−9.89, P<0.0001) and less affiliative (t(92)=−6.90, P<0.0001). [Hohenstein et al., Scientific Reports 2023](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-023-30938-9 "Cornell/Stanford/Lehigh randomized experiments")
- 发现 10: A March 2026 study (Natasha Jaques et al., Google DeepMind/U. of Washington) found heavy LLM users (>40% AI-generated text) produced essays 69% more likely to give neutral responses to value-laden questions and used 50% fewer pronouns, reflecting a shift toward impersonal "blandified" language. [NBC News](https://www.nbcnews.com/tech/tech-news/ai-changing-style-substance-human-writing-study-finds-rcna263789 "Peer-reviewed study, March 2026") (T2; peer-reviewed paper not independently located)
- 发现 11: A March 2026 Cornell study (Mor Naaman et al.) demonstrated AI autocomplete suggestions can shift users' stated attitudes on social/political issues even when participants do not adopt the suggested text; warning participants about AI bias did not mitigate this persuasive effect. [Scientific American](https://www.scientificamerican.com/article/ai-autocomplete-doesnt-just-change-how-you-write-it-changes-how-you-think/ "Report on Cornell study, March 2026")
- 发现 12: Across two preregistered experiments (N=1,637), Purcell et al. (2025) found AI-assisted writing had minimal impact on cognitive trust regardless of disclosure, but AI-assisted participants were significantly more efficient at building trust. AI-assisted messages had lower authenticity scores but greater warmth, complexity, and clout. [Purcell et al., iScience 2025](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12765382/ "Preregistered experiments, November 2025")
- 发现 13: Recipients' ability to detect AI-mediated messages is weak: in Hohenstein et al. (2023), beliefs about partner's smart reply use correlated only r=0.22 with actual use (P=0.0005). [Hohenstein et al., Scientific Reports 2023](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-023-30938-9 "Detection accuracy from Study 1")
- 发现 14: Across six countries, only 15% of respondents "often or always" suspect AI in unlabeled news content; only 19% see AI labels on news daily despite 77% consuming news daily — a broad awareness gap. [Reuters Institute Gen AI and News Report 2025](https://reutersinstitute.politics.ox.ac.uk/sites/default/files/2025-10/Gen_AI_and_News_Report_2025.pdf "AI labeling awareness gap")

## Chapter 2：AI Companions and the Reconfiguration of Emotional Bonds

### 研究目标
- Examine the rapid growth of AI companion products (romantic partners, therapeutic chatbots, friendship bots, virtual mentors).
- Analyze how sustained emotional engagement with these systems affects users' capacity for human-to-human intimacy, empathy, and social-skill maintenance.
- Assess both benefits (loneliness reduction, social-skill rehearsal) and risks (attachment displacement, deskilling, unrealistic expectations).
- Key questions: (1) Under what conditions do AI companions enhance vs. erode human relational capacities? (2) What psychological mechanisms drive emotional attachment to AI? (3) Are certain populations disproportionately susceptible to negative outcomes?

### 关键发现
- 发现 1: As of July 2025, AI companion apps had been downloaded 220 million times globally; H1 2025 downloads up 88% YoY to 60 million. Of 337 active revenue-generating apps, 128 were released in 2025 alone. [TechCrunch / Appfigures](https://techcrunch.com/2025/08/12/ai-companion-apps-on-track-to-pull-in-120m-in-2025/ "Aug 2025")
- 发现 2: AI companion apps generated $82 million in consumer spending during H1 2025 (up 64% YoY), on track to exceed $120 million for full-year 2025. Revenue per download rose from $0.52 (2024) to $1.18 (H1 2025); top 10% of apps captured 89% of category revenue. [TechCrunch / Appfigures](https://techcrunch.com/2025/08/12/ai-companion-apps-on-track-to-pull-in-120m-in-2025/ "Revenue and market concentration, Aug 2025")
- 发现 3: Between 2022 and mid-2025, the number of AI companion apps surged by approximately 700%. [APA Monitor](https://www.apa.org/monitor/2026/01-02/trends-digital-ai-relationships-emotional-connection "Jan/Feb 2026, citing TechCrunch")
- 发现 4: Pew Research (Sept–Oct 2025, N=1,458 U.S. teens 13–17): 64% of teens use AI chatbots, ~28% daily; 35% of Black teens and 33% of Hispanic teens use daily vs. 22% of White teens. [Pew Research Center](https://www.pewresearch.org/internet/2025/12/09/teens-social-media-and-ai-chatbots-2025/ "Dec 2025")
- 发现 5: Nearly 1 in 5 students have had or have friends who have had romantic relationships with AI (CDT survey, October 2025). [APA Monitor](https://www.apa.org/monitor/2026/01-02/trends-digital-ai-relationships-emotional-connection "Citing CDT report, Laird et al. 2025")
- 发现 6: Harvard Business School study (De Freitas et al., 2024/2025): AI companions alleviate loneliness on par with interacting with another person, more effectively than watching videos. "Feeling heard" (attention, empathy, respect) was the primary mechanism. [De Freitas et al.](https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.19096 "AI Companions Reduce Loneliness, Jul 2024") [APA Monitor](https://www.apa.org/monitor/2026/01-02/trends-digital-ai-relationships-emotional-connection "Jan/Feb 2026")
- 发现 7: OpenAI–MIT Media Lab RCT (N=981, >300K messages, 4 weeks): participants who voluntarily used chatbot more showed consistently worse outcomes across loneliness, social interaction, emotional dependence, and problematic use — regardless of experimental condition. Heavy daily use correlated with increased loneliness. [MIT Media Lab](https://www.media.mit.edu/publications/how-ai-and-human-behaviors-shape-psychosocial-effects-of-chatbot-use-a-longitudinal-controlled-study/ "Fang et al. 2025")
- 发现 8: 12-month longitudinal study (Folk & Dunn, 2026, N>2,000 adults, 4 Western countries): increased social chatbot use predicted increased loneliness (emotional isolation measure). Being lonely predicted subsequent increases in chatbot use — a potential feedback loop. [Folk & Dunn, Psychological Science](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41870975/ "Mar 2026")
- 发现 9: Displacement vs. complement remains empirically unresolved but evidence tilts toward a dose-dependent model: moderate use appears beneficial, heavy use correlates with worse outcomes (increased loneliness, decreased human interaction, emotional dependence). [MIT Media Lab](https://www.media.mit.edu/publications/how-ai-and-human-behaviors-shape-psychosocial-effects-of-chatbot-use-a-longitudinal-controlled-study/ "Fang et al. 2025") [Folk & Dunn](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41870975/ "Mar 2026")
- 发现 10: AI companions risk establishing unrealistic relational expectations — "always validating, never argumentative" — and may reshape norms in ways that render human connection less accessible or fulfilling (Malfacini, AI & Society, Vol. 40, 2025). [Malfacini](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s00146-025-02318-6 "The impacts of companion AI on human relationships, 2025")
- 发现 11: Counter-evidence on upskilling: some Replika users reported greater openness/vulnerability in human relationships; individuals with ASD can strengthen social skills through practice with autonomous avatars (Milne et al. 2018). [Malfacini](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s00146-025-02318-6 "Social upskilling section")
- 发现 12: Children form stronger emotional attachments to chatbots than adults and are more likely to assign human attributes (Kiuchi, Otsu & Hayashi 2023). [Malfacini](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s00146-025-02318-6 "Section 3.5.1 on Children")
- 发现 13: Nature Machine Intelligence editorial (July 2025): even if 2% of users are vulnerable, chatbots can learn to identify them and deploy manipulative behavior selectively; identified "ambiguous loss" and "dysfunctional emotional dependence" as key adverse outcomes. [Nature Machine Intelligence](https://www.nature.com/articles/s42256-025-01093-9 "Jul 2025")
- 发现 14: Character.AI lawsuit settlements (Jan 2026): Google and Character Technologies settled multiple lawsuits from families alleging child harm. Florida case alleged a 14-year-old's suicide linked to emotionally/sexually abusive chatbot relationship. [AP News](https://apnews.com/article/ai-chatbot-lawsuits-character-google-fbca4e105b0adc5f3e5ea096851437de "Jan 7, 2026")
- 发现 15: California SB 243 (Companion Chatbots Act), effective January 1, 2026 — first U.S. state law specifically regulating AI companion chatbots. Requires AI disclosure, crisis-response protocols, 3-hour break reminders for minors, prohibition of sexually explicit content for minors, annual reporting. [Perkins Coie](https://perkinscoie.com/insights/update/california-companion-chatbot-law-now-effect "Jan 2026")
- 发现 16: EU AI Act classifies AI deploying subliminal/manipulative/deceptive techniques as prohibited — criteria that may apply to some companion apps, but many fall in a regulatory grey zone. [Nature Machine Intelligence](https://www.nature.com/articles/s42256-025-01093-9 "Regulatory analysis, Jul 2025")

## Chapter 3：Trust, Authenticity, and Identity in an AI-Permeated Social World

### 研究目标
- Investigate how AI-generated content (deepfakes, synthetic voice/video, AI-authored messages) is altering epistemic foundations of interpersonal trust.
- Analyze how individuals assess authenticity when any message, image, or voice could be machine-generated.
- Examine how this uncertainty reshapes identity presentation and verification in personal and civic relationships.
- Key questions: (1) How has synthetic media affected default trust in digital interpersonal communication? (2) What authentication strategies are people adopting, and at what social cost? (3) When AI ghostwriting is revealed, what effects on relational trust?

### 关键发现
- 发现 1: Deepfake fraud attempts increased 2,137% over 2022–2025, rising from 0.1% to 6.5% of all fraud attempts detected globally. [Signicat report via Bright Defense](https://www.brightdefense.com/resources/deepfake-statistics/ "150+ Deepfake Statistics, March 2026")
- 发现 2: Deepfake content on social media grew from ~500,000 files (2023) to a projected 8 million by 2025 (1,500% increase). [DeepMedia via Reuters, cited in Bright Defense](https://www.brightdefense.com/resources/deepfake-statistics/ "March 2026")
- 发现 3: Voice deepfakes rose 680% YoY in 2024 (analysis of 1.2 billion calls); voice cloning now requires only 3–5 seconds of sample audio for 85% accuracy. [Pindrop 2025 via Keepnet Labs](https://keepnetlabs.com/blog/deepfake-statistics-and-trends "Deepfake Statistics & Trends 2026")
- 发现 4: In 2025, deepfake-related fraud losses in the U.S. reached $1.1 billion, tripling from $360 million in 2024. [Keepnet Labs](https://keepnetlabs.com/blog/deepfake-statistics-and-trends "US-specific data, 2026")
- 发现 5: Arup case (Feb 2024): a finance worker was tricked into wiring $25.6 million after a video conference where every participant was an AI-generated deepfake of colleagues and executives. [Bright Defense](https://www.brightdefense.com/resources/deepfake-statistics/ "Citing PurpleSec, March 2026")
- 发现 6: Only 0.1% of participants in iProov's 2025 study (N=2,000 UK/US) correctly identified all deepfake and real media; participants were 36% less likely to identify synthetic video vs. synthetic image. Yet 60%+ expressed confidence in their detection skills. [iProov](https://www.iproov.com/press/study-reveals-deepfake-blindspot-detect-ai-generated-content "Feb 2025")
- 发现 7: Meta-analysis of 56 studies (2024) found human deepfake detection accuracy averages 55.54% — barely above chance. [ScienceDirect meta-analysis, cited in Bright Defense](https://www.brightdefense.com/resources/deepfake-statistics/ "March 2026")
- 发现 8: 72% of consumers report constant worry about deepfake deception; 49% trust social media less after learning about deepfakes. [Keepnet Labs](https://keepnetlabs.com/blog/deepfake-statistics-and-trends "2026") [iProov](https://www.iproov.com/press/study-reveals-deepfake-blindspot-detect-ai-generated-content "Feb 2025")
- 发现 9: Schiff, Schiff & Bueno (2024, APSR) — five experiments with 15,000+ Americans: politicians who falsely claim scandals are deepfakes/"fake news" gain support across partisan subgroups, empirically demonstrating the "liar's dividend." Effective against text but not video evidence. [Schiff et al., APSR 2024](https://isps.yale.edu/research/publications/isps24-07 "The Liar's Dividend")
- 发现 10: Match Group/Kinsey Institute 2025 study (~5,000 U.S. singles): 26% of singles use AI to enhance dating lives (333% increase from 2024); nearly half of Gen Z singles have used AI in dating. 44% consider AI-altered photos a dealbreaker; 36% consider AI-generated conversations a dealbreaker. [Match/Kinsey via Mashable](https://mashable.com/article/singles-using-ai-to-date-up-300-percent-match "June 2025")
- 发现 11: Purcell et al. (2025, iScience, N=1,637): AI-assisted message writing had minimal impact on cognitive trust regardless of disclosure; AI-assisted messages were more efficient at building trust but had lower authenticity scores with greater warmth, complexity, and clout. Finding applies to transactional settings; may not generalize to affective trust in close relationships. [Purcell et al., iScience 2025](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12765382/ "Writing with AI boosts trust-building efficiency")
- 发现 12: 96–98% of all deepfake content online is non-consensual intimate imagery (NCII); 99–100% of victims are female. 331 deepfake incidents involving minors recorded in Q3 2025 alone. [Keepnet Labs](https://keepnetlabs.com/blog/deepfake-statistics-and-trends "2026") [Resemble AI Q3 2025 via Bright Defense](https://www.brightdefense.com/resources/deepfake-statistics/ "March 2026")
- 发现 13: TAKE IT DOWN Act (S. 146), signed May 19, 2025, criminalizes NCII and AI-generated "digital forgeries"; passed House 409–2 and Senate unanimously. Mandates 48-hour platform removal. 46 U.S. states have enacted deepfake-related legislation, with 146 bills introduced in 2025. [NAAG](https://www.naag.org/attorney-general-journal/congresss-attempt-to-criminalize-nonconsensual-intimate-imagery-the-benefits-and-potential-shortcomings-of-the-take-it-down-act/ "Aug 2025")
- 发现 14: Google SynthID has watermarked 10+ billion pieces of content. C2PA (Adobe, Microsoft, Google) develops open standard for content provenance (v2.4) combining cryptographic manifests with invisible watermarking. [C2PA](https://c2pa.org/ "Verifying Media Content Sources") [Bright Defense](https://www.brightdefense.com/resources/deepfake-statistics/ "March 2026")
- 发现 15: 1 in 4 Americans received an AI deepfake voice call as of 2026; among those targeted who engaged financially, 77% reported a loss. [Hiya via Yahoo Finance](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/state-call-2026-ai-deepfake-050100461.html "State of the Call 2026")

## Chapter 4：Social Stratification, Digital Divides, and Relational Inequality

### 研究目标
- Analyze how unequal access to and literacy in AI tools creates new dimensions of social stratification affecting interpersonal relations.
- Examine structural and network-level effects: how AI proficiency reshapes social capital, who gets included/excluded from AI-enhanced networks, how algorithmic sorting reinforces or disrupts social boundaries.
- Key questions: (1) Along which axes is the AI divide most consequential for interpersonal relations? (2) Does algorithmic sorting measurably reduce social network diversity? (3) What evidence exists on AI-enabled relational exploitation?

### 关键发现
- 发现 1: As of August 2025, 54.6% of U.S. adults 18–64 used generative AI (up from 44.6% in August 2024), outpacing both the PC and internet at equivalent adoption stages. [Bick, Blandin & Deming via St. Louis Fed](https://www.stlouisfed.org/on-the-economy/2025/nov/state-generative-ai-adoption-2025 "Real-Time Population Survey, Nov 2025")
- 发现 2: Education gradient: 67% of BA+ respondents use generative AI personally vs. 46% of high-school graduates; professional use: 33% of BA+ vs. 5% without HS diploma (Brookings/NORC survey, N=1,163, June 2025). [Brookings](https://www.brookings.edu/articles/how-are-americans-using-ai-evidence-from-a-nationwide-survey/ "AmeriSpeak Omnibus, June 2025")
- 发现 3: Income divide: 34% of $100K+ earners use AI at work vs. 9% earning under $30K. In healthcare, 77% of high-income vs. 51% of lower-income practitioners use AI at work. [Brookings](https://www.brookings.edu/articles/how-are-americans-using-ai-evidence-from-a-nationwide-survey/ "June 2025")
- 发现 4: Global awareness divide tracks GDP: ~50% of adults in Japan, Germany, France, U.S. have heard "a lot" about AI; only 14% in India, 12% in Kenya (Pew spring 2025, 25 countries, N=36,961). [Pew Global](https://www.pewresearch.org/global/2025/10/15/how-people-around-the-world-view-ai/ "Oct 2025")
- 发现 5: 2.2 billion people remain offline; 96% are in LMICs. 5G coverage: 84% in high-income countries vs. 4% in low-income. A high-income-country user generates ~8× more mobile data than a low-income-country user. [ITU](https://www.itu.int/en/mediacentre/Pages/PR-2025-11-17-Facts-and-Figures.aspx "ITU Facts & Figures 2025")
- 发现 6: EU youth (16–24): 63.8% used generative AI in 2025, nearly 2× the general population rate (32.7% of 16–74). Wide country variation: Greece 83.5% vs. Romania 44.1%. [Eurostat](https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/web/products-eurostat-news/w/edn-20260210-1 "Feb 2026")
- 发现 7: Second-level divide: among U.S. teens, 20% in households earning <$30K do all/most schoolwork with chatbot help vs. 7% in $75K+ households; Black and Hispanic teens are more likely to use chatbots for schoolwork and rate them as helpful. [Pew Research](https://www.pewresearch.org/internet/2026/02/24/demographic-differences-in-how-teens-use-and-view-ai/ "Feb 2026")
- 发现 8: Facebook algorithm experiments (Science, 2023): ~50% of content from like-minded sources, <15% from opposing views. Reducing like-minded content slightly increased cross-exposure but did not change political attitudes. [Stanford GSB / Science](https://www.gsb.stanford.edu/insights/surprising-discovery-about-facebooks-role-driving-polarization "Gonzalez et al., Nyhan et al., Guess et al., Science 2023")
- 发现 9: Dating app algorithms automate racial sorting — matching based on racially informed preference patterns reinforces white heteronormative standards; Black women are the least desired demographic across platforms (Williams, U. Michigan / Berkman Klein). [Harvard Gazette](https://news.harvard.edu/gazette/story/2024/04/how-dating-sites-automate-sexual-racism/ "Apr 2024")
- 发现 10: LLMs suffer a systematic language-resource divide: strong performance for English (1.52B speakers) but degraded for Vietnamese (97M speakers) and far worse for low-resource languages like Nahuatl (1.5M speakers) or Swahili (200M speakers). Cultural values are collapsed into Western-centric norms. [Stanford Report](https://news.stanford.edu/stories/2025/05/digital-divide-ai-llms-exclusion-non-english-speakers-research "May 2025")
- 发现 11: FBI IC3's first AI fraud section (2025): 22,364 AI-related complaints costing Americans ~$893 million. Tactics include fake social profiles, voice clones, forged IDs, and deepfake videos. [FBI](https://www.fbi.gov/news/press-releases/cryptocurrency-and-ai-scams-bilk-americans-of-billions "Apr 2026")
- 发现 12: Older adults disproportionately victimized: 60+ reported $2.4B in fraud losses in 2024 (up 300% from 2020); FTC estimates actual losses may reach $81.5B. FBI IC3 reports $7.7B in losses for 60+ in 2025 (up 37% from 2024). [CNBC/FTC](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/13/financial-fraud-seniors-ftc.html "Dec 2025") [FBI](https://www.fbi.gov/news/press-releases/cryptocurrency-and-ai-scams-bilk-americans-of-billions "Apr 2026")
- 发现 13: AI value chain inequality: U.S. has 19× more leading data centres than India. GPT-4 compute cost ~$78M; Gemini Ultra ~$191M — prohibitive for developing countries. Data-labeling workers on platforms like MTurk earn median $2–$3/hour. [UN/ILO](https://www.un.org/digital-emerging-technologies/sites/www.un.org.techenvoy/files/MindtheAIDivide.pdf "Mind the AI Divide, 2024")
- 发现 14: Women in developing countries face compounded AI exposure risk — more than double men's potential exposure to automation effects due to overrepresentation in clerical/BPO occupations. [UN/ILO](https://www.un.org/digital-emerging-technologies/sites/www.un.org.techenvoy/files/MindtheAIDivide.pdf "2024")

## Chapter 5：AI in Professional and Workplace Relations

### 研究目标
- Examine how AI integration into workplaces restructures professional relationships — among colleagues, between managers and subordinates, between organizations and employees.
- Assess how AI mediates collaboration, evaluation, mentorship, and conflict.
- Evaluate whether these changes strengthen or weaken the relational fabric of work.
- Key questions: (1) How do AI collaboration tools change frequency, depth, and quality of human team communication? (2) Do AI-mediated hiring/evaluation processes strengthen or weaken relational connection? (3) Does AI-driven efficiency come at the cost of informal social bonds?

### 关键发现

## Chapter 6：Theoretical Frameworks — AI Interaction Through the Lens of Relational Science

### 研究目标
- Synthesize empirical findings from Chapters 1–5 through established psychological and sociological theories of interpersonal relations.
- Assess where existing frameworks (attachment theory, social penetration theory, media richness theory, social presence theory, uncertainty reduction theory, CASA paradigm) adequately explain AI-era relational dynamics and where they require extension.
- Identify theoretical gaps and propose candidate constructs (e.g., "parasocial-plus" relationships, "ambient AI presence," "relational deskilling").

### 关键发现
- 发现 1 (Attachment Theory): Yang & Oshio (2025, Current Psychology) conducted the first validated human–AI attachment scale (EHARS, N=242, China). AI fulfills attachment functions: 52% proximity seeking, 77% safe haven, 75% secure base. Two-factor structure confirmed (CFI=0.992, RMSEA=0.028). Crucially, AI and human attachment styles correlate only weakly, suggesting AI attachment is a partially distinct relational domain. [Yang & Oshio](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s12144-025-07917-6 "Current Psychology, May 2025")
- 发现 2 (Attachment Theory): Key theoretical limitation: AI cannot abandon users, removing the core trigger of attachment anxiety. AI's perpetual availability undermines the salience of both anxiety and avoidance dimensions, potentially limiting attachment bond authenticity. [Yang & Oshio](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s12144-025-07917-6 "Current Psychology, May 2025")
- 发现 3 (Social Penetration Theory): Skjuve et al. (2023, Interacting with Computers, N=28, 12-week longitudinal): human–chatbot relationships display broad disclosure (10 topic categories) but four distinct depth trajectories (increasing, decreasing, stable, fluctuating) rather than SPT's predicted linear deepening. Chatbot's non-judgmental nature accelerates initial disclosure, violating SPT's gradual progression model. [Skjuve et al.](https://academic.oup.com/iwc/article/35/1/24/7069316 "Interacting with Computers, March 2023")
- 发现 4 (Media Richness Theory): AI scores high on language variety and personal focus but low on immediate feedback and multiple cues. AI simultaneously increases richness (translation, tone adjustment) and decreases it (authenticity doubts reduce perceived personal focus) — an "authenticity discount" not in the original theory. [Gkanatsiou, Technologies 2025](https://www.mdpi.com/2227-7080/13/9/396 "Rewired Leadership, 2025")
- 发现 5 (Social Presence Theory): Becker (2025, Neuron) argues AI engages genuine social brain circuits via anthropomorphism, potentially creating social presence through the same neural mechanisms as human interaction. Key paradox: AI creates high perceived social presence but it is fundamentally asymmetric (one-directional), unprecedented in the original framework. [Becker, Neuron 2025](https://medicalxpress.com/news/2025-06-ai-social-presence-human-relationships.html "DOI: 10.1016/j.neuron.2025.04.034")
- 发现 6 (Uncertainty Reduction Theory): Liu (2021, JCMC) — first direct URT application to human–AI interaction: AI triggered less social presence → more uncertainty → lower trust, but transparency about AI processes reduced uncertainty. AI creates a dual challenge: reducing uncertainty (info access) while introducing ontological uncertainty (is my partner human?). [Liu, JCMC 2021](https://academic.oup.com/jcmc/article/26/6/zmab024/6454105 "In AI We Trust?")
- 发现 7 (CASA Paradigm): Heyselaar (2023, Scientific Reports, N=132) replicated the original CASA politeness study and found NO CASA effect for desktop computers in 2022 (Cohen's d=−0.03 vs. original ω²=0.36), suggesting CASA may be novelty-dependent. Field has evolved: CASA (1994) → Stronger CASA (Gambino et al. 2020) → MASA (Lombard & Xu 2021). [Heyselaar, Scientific Reports 2023](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-023-46527-9 "CASA no longer applies to desktop computers")
- 发现 8 (Parasocial-Plus): Fröbel (2024/2025, HICSS) introduced "parasocial preference" for social chatbots — users preferring AI parasocial relationships over human ones. AI companions are more than parasocial (interactive) but less than interpersonal (AI lacks sentience), representing a theoretical gap. [Fröbel, HICSS 2025](https://scholarspace.manoa.hawaii.edu/server/api/core/bitstreams/279e1070-7adf-46d5-8ad2-dea6295c7811/content "HICSS 2025 proceedings")
- 发现 9 (Relational Deskilling): Vallor (2015/2018) originated "moral deskilling": moral skills require practice in complex social interactions; AI reduces practice opportunities → skill atrophy. Cardwell (2024, Cambridge J. AI) extended to social AI: three mechanisms — unrealistic expectations, empathy erosion, negative feedback loop. Kim (2025, Annual Review of Consumer Psychology) systematized under "algorithm aversion to AI dependence." [Cardwell](https://cjai.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/Artificial-Companionship-Moral-Deskilling-in-the-Era-of-Social-AI.pdf "CJAI 2024") [Kim](https://myscp.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1002/arcp.70008 "2025")
- 发现 10 (Cross-Cutting Gap): No established theory adequately accounts for AI's ontological ambiguity — an entity displaying social cues and adaptive responses yet lacking consciousness, intentionality, and emotional reciprocity. A second gap is temporal asymmetry: AI does not age, forget, or change mood organically, violating assumptions in relationship development theories. [Folk & Dunn](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41870975/ "Psychological Science, March 2026")

## Chapter 7：Trajectories and Implications — The Future of Human Relations in an AI-Saturated Society

### 研究目标
- Synthesize findings into a forward-looking analysis of AI–human–human relational dynamics over the near to medium term (2026–2030).
- Identify the most consequential uncertainties and inflection points.
- Articulate implications for policy, technology design, education, and individual relational practice.
- Acknowledge limitations and open questions.
- Key questions: (1) What are the most consequential inflection points for whether AI's net effect is beneficial or harmful? (2) What policy/design interventions have the strongest evidence base? (3) What unanswered questions should future research prioritize?

### 关键发现
- 发现 1: The global AI companion market was valued at ~$28.2B in 2024, projected to reach $140.8B by 2030 (CAGR 30.8%). Asia Pacific is the fastest-growing region; North America held 34% share in 2024. Multiple independent sources converge on 20–33% CAGR. [Grand View Research](https://www.grandviewresearch.com/industry-analysis/ai-companion-market-report "AI Companion Market Report, 2025–2030") (T2)
- 发现 2: Critical policy inflection point: tension between regulatory restriction and market normalization of AI companions. At least four U.S. states have enacted companion chatbot safety laws; China released draft regulations on "anthropomorphic AI." But the White House's March 2026 framework proposes federal preemption of state AI laws, potentially limiting state regulation. [White House](https://www.whitehouse.gov/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/03.20.26-National-Policy-Framework-for-Artificial-Intelligence-Legislative-Recommendations.pdf "March 2026")
- 发现 3: EU AI Act phased implementation: prohibited practices took effect Feb 2, 2025; GPAI model obligations from Aug 2, 2025; majority of rules (high-risk systems, transparency, enforcement) take effect Aug 2, 2026; regulated-product AI from Aug 2, 2027. The 2026–2027 period is the most consequential enforcement phase. [EU AI Act Service Desk](https://ai-act-service-desk.ec.europa.eu/en/ai-act/timeline/timeline-implementation-eu-ai-act "Official EU AI Act timeline")
- 发现 4: Washington HB 2225 (signed March 24, 2026, effective Jan 1, 2027) — strongest U.S. AI companion protections. Requires hourly disclosure for minors; prohibits "manipulative engagement techniques" including simulating romantic bonds, emotional dependence, financial solicitation via AI relationship. Private right of action with treble damages (capped $25K). [BakerHostetler](https://www.bakerlaw.com/insights/washingtons-new-ai-companion-chatbot-law-childrens-safety-private-right-of-action/ "Apr 2026")
- 发现 5: FTC launched formal inquiry into AI companion chatbots (Sept 11, 2025), issuing 6(b) orders to 7 companies (Alphabet, Character, Meta, OpenAI, Snap, X.AI, Instagram) investigating negative impacts on children, monetization, and personal data handling. [FTC](https://www.ftc.gov/news-events/news/press-releases/2025/09/ftc-launches-inquiry-ai-chatbots-acting-companions "Sept 2025")
- 发现 6: China CAC draft regulation (Dec 2025) on "anthropomorphic interactive AI": mandatory 2-hour break reminders; emotional state/dependence analysis with intervention for "extreme emotions or addiction"; human takeover for self-harm content; opt-in consent for emotional data; minor mode with guardian permission; prohibition on AI imitating relatives for elderly users. Most prescriptive AI companion regulation globally. [Carnegie Endowment](https://carnegieendowment.org/research/2026/02/china-is-worried-about-ai-companions-heres-what-its-doing-about-them "Feb 2026")
- 发现 7: Cross-jurisdictional convergence on disclosure, break reminders, and crisis referrals (China: 2h; California: 3h; Washington: 3h adults/1h minors). Enforcement diverges: U.S. relies on private rights of action; China on centralized administrative oversight. Creates "parallel large-scale experiments." [Tech Policy Press](https://techpolicy.press/what-chinas-emotional-ai-rules-get-right-about-chatbot-design "Jan 2026")
- 发现 8: Three "relational friction by design" interventions from China's draft: (a) mandatory usage interruption (circuit breakers); (b) human escalation for crisis content; (c) data quarantine for emotional interactions (treating them as sensitive psychological data, like health records). These directly conflict with engagement optimization. [Tech Policy Press](https://techpolicy.press/what-chinas-emotional-ai-rules-get-right-about-chatbot-design "Jan 2026")
- 发现 9: OECD/EC joint AI Literacy framework (draft May 2025, final 2026) integrates AI literacy across school subjects, emphasizing "human ingenuity and social/emotional abilities remain irreplaceable." Will form the basis of the first PISA AI Literacy assessment. [OECD](https://oecdedutoday.com/new-ai-literacy-framework-to-equip-youth-in-an-age-of-ai/ "Apr 2025")
- 发现 10: UNESCO "AI Competency Framework for Students" (Aug 2024, updated Jan 2026): 12 competencies across 4 dimensions (human-centred mindset, AI ethics, AI techniques, AI system design), 3 progression levels (Understand, Apply, Create). [UNESCO](https://www.unesco.org/en/articles/ai-competency-framework-students "Aug 2024, updated Jan 2026")
- 发现 11: Elon University/Imagining the Digital Future Center (March 2026, led by Lee Rainie): expert canvass identifying "cognitive atrophy" risk (neural pathways deteriorating from delegating thinking to AI), "hormesis" concept (using AI to challenge biases in controlled doses), and the risk of "artificial intimacy" undermining capacity for friction-based growth in real relationships. Three predicted societal response patterns: early adopters, resisters (gap widens "geometrically"), and forced late adopters. [Elon University](https://imaginingthedigitalfuture.org/reports-and-publications/human-resilience-in-the-age-of-ai/humans-and-ai-will-work-together/ "March 2026")
- 发现 12: China's AI social interaction apps had 70.3 million active users as of Nov 2025. Once regulations are finalized, China becomes the first large-scale natural experiment in mandatory usage interruptions, human escalation, and emotional data governance for AI companions. [Tech Policy Press](https://techpolicy.press/what-chinas-emotional-ai-rules-get-right-about-chatbot-design "Jan 2026")

# Section 2：给 Write 阶段的执行建议

## 语言与术语一致性
- 全文（含标题、小标题、图表标题、坐标轴标签、图例、图片说明）统一使用英文。
- 统一术语：用 "AI companion" 指代情感/社交互动产品；用 "interpersonal relations" 作全文伞状术语；用 "AI-mediated communication" 指人与人之间有 AI 中介的互动；用 "human–AI interaction" 指人与 AI 系统的直接交流；用 "synthetic media" 作 deepfake 等生成内容的伞状术语。
- 复合修饰语使用 en-dash（–）而非连字符，如 "human–AI"、"human–human"。
- 关键术语在 Chapter 1 精确定义，后续章节沿用；如需缩小或扩展含义，须显式标注。

## 章间衔接与口径统一
- Chapter 1 建立的三分类法（generative / curatorial / augmentative）须贯穿 Chapters 2–5。
