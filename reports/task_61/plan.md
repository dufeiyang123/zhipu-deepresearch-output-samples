# Section 1：章节研究计划

## Chapter 1：Biology, Fishery Significance, and Production Overview of Chub Mackerel across the Pacific Rim

### 研究目标
- What is the taxonomic identity, life history, and migratory ecology of *Scomber japonicus* across distinct Pacific Rim stocks (Pacific stock, Tsushima Warm Current stock, East China Sea stock, Southeast Pacific stock, U.S. West Coast stock)?
- What are the total and country-level landings over the past decade (approximately 2015–2025), and how does chub mackerel rank in commercial importance among small pelagic species in each major fishing nation (Japan, South Korea, China, Chile, Peru, Russia Far East, USA)?
- What are the major stock units recognized by NPFC and national agencies, their spawning grounds, seasonal migration routes, and how do these biological attributes set the stage for subsequent price and body-condition analyses?

### 关键发现
- *Scomber japonicus* (Houttuyn, 1782) is restricted to the Indo-Pacific; Atlantic populations are now classified as *S. colias* (COI divergence ~2%). In the U.S. it is commonly called "Pacific mackerel" [FishBase](https://www.fishbase.se/summary/Scomber-japonicus.html "Scomber japonicus species summary") [Trucco & Buratti 2017](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC5640896/ "Taxonomic review via phylogenetic analysis").
- Maximum TL 64 cm, weight 2.9 kg, max age 18 years; common FL ~30 cm; generation time 3.6 years [FishBase](https://www.fishbase.se/summary/Scomber-japonicus.html "FishBase species summary").
- Von Bertalanffy parameters differ by stock: NPFC assessment uses L∞=371 mm FL, K=0.39, t₀=−1.96, M=0.41; Zhejiang coastal TWC stock L∞=283 mm FL, K=0.36; East Yellow Sea L∞=405 mm FL, K=0.49 [Cai et al. 2023](https://www.mdpi.com/2410-3888/8/2/80 "Multi-Model Stock Assessment") [Cai et al. 2022](https://www.mdpi.com/2077-1312/10/2/301 "Growth Heterogeneity of Chub Mackerel").
- U.S. West Coast stock: L₅₀=274±1.26 mm FL, A₅₀=1.01±0.06 yr (n=911 females, 2010–2021 surveys) [NOAA TM NMFS-SWFSC-689](https://repository.library.noaa.gov/view/noaa/52097/noaa_52097_DS1.pdf "Life history for 2023 benchmark assessment").
- Multiple-batch spawner, indeterminate fecundity (100K–400K eggs/female), spawning at 15–20°C (optimum ~18°C). NW Pacific spawning Jan–Jun peaking Mar–Apr at Izu Islands (Pacific stock) and East China Sea/Tsushima Strait (TWC stock) [FishBase](https://www.fishbase.se/summary/Scomber-japonicus.html "Spawning data") [FRA Japan 2019](https://www.fra.go.jp/shigen/fisheries_resources/meeting/peer_review_meeting/files/2020/assess_masaba_p.pdf "FRA Pacific stock assessment").
- Two principal NW Pacific stocks: Pacific stock (Pacific coast of Japan to ~170°E) and TWC stock (East China Sea, Yellow Sea, Sea of Japan); additionally SE Pacific stock (Chile/Peru) and U.S. West Coast stock [Wang et al. 2022](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/marine-science/articles/10.3389/fmars.2022.996626/full "Climate-induced variation in TSI").
- Pacific stock migration: spawning at Izu Islands (Mar–Apr) → northward feeding migration to Sanriku/Hokkaido → southward wintering off Chiba/Ibaraki. High-abundance range extends to 47°N/166°E [FRA Japan 2019](https://www.fra.go.jp/shigen/fisheries_resources/meeting/peer_review_meeting/files/2020/assess_masaba_p.pdf "FRA Pacific stock — Distribution/migration").
- NPFC area total catch ~460,238 t in 2020 (≈34% of global production). Japan Pacific stock peak ~539,260 t (FY2017); Japan catch 298,331 t, China 129,868 t, Russia 98,373 t in FY2018 [Cai et al. 2023](https://www.mdpi.com/2410-3888/8/2/80 "Stock Assessment — Introduction") [FRA Japan 2019](https://www.fra.go.jp/shigen/fisheries_resources/meeting/peer_review_meeting/files/2020/assess_masaba_p.pdf "FRA Table 3-1").
- Japan Pacific stock catch shows dramatic historical fluctuations: peak ~1,207,000 t (FY1978), trough ~27,767 t (FY1990), recovery to ~332,000 t (FY2015–2017). Significant catch declines in NPFC area in 2022–2024 [FRA Japan 2019](https://www.fra.go.jp/shigen/fisheries_resources/meeting/peer_review_meeting/files/2020/assess_masaba_p.pdf "FRA Table 3-1") [NPFC CMM 2024-07](https://www.npfc.int/system/files/2025-03/NPFC-2025-COM09-WP04%20Rev.1%20CMM%202024-07%20for%20Chub%20Mackerel%20By%20Japan_0.pdf "Japan proposal noting catch declines").
- U.S./Mexico combined catch ranged 3,331–32,027 mt (2009–2020); 2023 total 15,866 mt (Mexico ~14,980 mt, U.S. ~885 mt). U.S. commercial landings far below harvest guidelines (7–39% utilized, 2008–2023) [Jensen et al. 2025](https://www.pcouncil.org/documents/2025/05/catch-only-stock-assessment-of-pacific-mackerel-scomber-japonicus-for-u-s-management-in-the-2025-26-and-2026-27-fishing-years.pdf/ "NOAA TM NMFS-SWFSC-718").
- Russia resumed chub mackerel fishing from ~2014, escalating from 36 t (FY2014) to 98,373 t (FY2018); ~87,388 t in 2021 [FRA Japan 2019](https://www.fra.go.jp/shigen/fisheries_resources/meeting/peer_review_meeting/files/2020/assess_masaba_p.pdf "FRA Table 3-1") [FAO AGRIS](https://agris.fao.org/search/en/providers/122436/records/67599d0cc7a957febdfe6824 "CPUE standardization for Russian fleet").
- NPFC CMM 2024-07 sets Convention Area catch limit at 100,000 mt for 2024 and 2025 calendar years, with Chinese Taipei TAC of 66,740 t [NPFC 2025 Stock Assessment Report](https://www.npfc.int/system/files/2025-04/Stock%20assessment%20report%20for%20chub%20mackerel.pdf "NPFC Executive Summary").
- Multi-model assessment (2023 study, 2020 reference year): MSY estimated at 400,000–660,000 t; JABBA model indicated 88.3% probability of healthy stock status (B₂₀₂₀/B_MSY=1.40–2.30) [Cai et al. 2023](https://www.mdpi.com/2410-3888/8/2/80 "Stock Assessment Results/Discussion").
- FRA Japan estimated Pacific stock biomass at 5,595,000 t in FY2018 (highest since 1970), SSB 1,185,000 t; SB_MSY=1,545,000 t, MSY=372,000 t; SSB/SB_MSY=0.77, F/F_MSY=2.48 [FRA Japan 2019](https://www.fra.go.jp/shigen/fisheries_resources/meeting/peer_review_meeting/files/2020/assess_masaba_p.pdf "FRA Section 4/Summary").
- U.S. West Coast 2025 assessment: age-1+ biomass 61,737 mt (2025–26), 67,954 mt (2026–27); above 18,200 mt cutoff. HG 9,143 mt (2025–26), 10,448 mt (2026–27) [Jensen et al. 2025](https://www.pcouncil.org/documents/2025/05/catch-only-stock-assessment-of-pacific-mackerel-scomber-japonicus-for-u-s-management-in-the-2025-26-and-2026-27-fishing-years.pdf/ "NOAA TM NMFS-SWFSC-718 Table 4").
- Density-dependent growth documented: average body weight of Pacific stock notably lower in FY2018 vs. 2011–2014, especially for 2013 year class; attributed to high abundance and offshore distribution to poorer feeding areas [FRA Japan 2019](https://www.fra.go.jp/shigen/fisheries_resources/meeting/peer_review_meeting/files/2020/assess_masaba_p.pdf "FRA — Age and growth section").
- Spawning TSI experienced regime shifts: 1979/1980 in Pacific coast (coinciding with stock collapse), late 1990s in East China Sea/Tsushima (coinciding with TWC stock decline); driven by Siberian High and Aleutian Low/ENSO [Wang et al. 2022](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/marine-science/articles/10.3389/fmars.2022.996626/full "Climate-induced TSI variation").
- Relative condition factors of NW Pacific chub mackerel (Chinese vessel data, 2016–2020) declined compared to pre-2006 values; lowest in 2017; Zhejiang coastal (TWC) fish heavier at same FL than high-seas Pacific stock fish [Cai et al. 2022](https://www.mdpi.com/2077-1312/10/2/301 "Growth Heterogeneity — Condition factors").

## Chapter 3：Interannual Variations in Body Size — Weight, Length, and Condition Factor across Fishing Grounds

### 研究目标
- How have mean body weight and fork length at age (particularly age-0 and age-1+) changed interannually across the major fishing grounds (Northwest Pacific, East China Sea, Tsushima Strait, Southeast Pacific) over the past 5–10 years?
- Is there evidence of declining or increasing body condition, and does this correlate with stock abundance levels (density-dependent growth)?
- How do spatial differences in body size relate to regional oceanographic conditions?
- Present length-weight relationships, condition factor time series, and size-frequency distributions to establish biological quality metrics linking market value and environmental conditions.

### 关键发现
- NW Pacific 整体体长-体重关系（2016–2020，n=2,686）：W = 1.41×10⁻⁶ × FL³·³⁷（正异速生长），参数 a 低于历史值，可能反映栖息地退化 [Cai et al. 2022](https://www.mdpi.com/2077-1312/10/2/301 "Growth Heterogeneity of Chub Mackerel, JMSE")。FRA Japan 太平洋种群数据（n=18,302, 2006–2019）：W = 1.09×10⁻⁶ × FL³·⁴¹ [Kamimura et al. 2021](https://academic.oup.com/icesjms/article/78/9/3254/6380055 "ICES J. Mar. Sci.")。
- VBGF 参数因种群和年级差异显著：太平洋种群 2006–2016 年级 L∞ 从 440.5 mm（2006 年级）降至 339.9 mm（2016 年级），K 范围 0.25–0.55；1 龄增长率 29.6–51.2 mm/yr [Kamimura et al. 2021](https://academic.oup.com/icesjms/article/78/9/3254/6380055 "ICES J. Mar. Sci. — cohort-specific VBGF")。
- 太平洋种群 0 龄鱼平均叉长：高丰度期（1975）仅 16.9 cm，低丰度期（1989）达 25.9 cm；28 年均值 21.7±2.1 cm。0 龄体型劣势终身保持——0 龄 FL 与 1/3/4 龄 FL 正相关（r=0.83/0.62/0.67, P<0.05）[Watanabe & Yatsu 2004](https://spo.nmfs.noaa.gov/sites/default/files/pdf-content/2004/1021/watanabe.pdf "Fisheries Bulletin 102:196–206")。
- 修正 VBGF 模型纳入种群密度和 SST（4–6 月, 38–40°N）：k(i,y)=0.271−0.008×T−0.21×D，密度效应大于 SST 效应；种群从 590 万吨（1977）崩溃至 20 万吨（1990）时产生了时序中最大个体 [Watanabe & Yatsu 2004](https://spo.nmfs.noaa.gov/sites/default/files/pdf-content/2004/1021/watanabe.pdf "Tables 5–6")。
- 太平洋种群 1 龄+ 相对肥满度（Kn）2006–2018 年总体下降趋势，随同种和异种（沙丁鱼）丰度增加而降低 [Kamimura et al. 2021](https://academic.oup.com/icesjms/article/78/9/3254/6380055 "ICES J. Mar. Sci. — Figure 5/6")。NW Pacific 2016–2020 年相对肥满度普遍低于 1（相对历史参考期），2017 年最低；浙江近海（TWC 种群）同等叉长下体重高于日本近海（太平洋种群）[Cai et al. 2022](https://www.mdpi.com/2077-1312/10/2/301 "Growth Heterogeneity — Table 4")。
- SEM 模型证实强密度依赖效应：鯖鱼自身丰度（Nm）对 Kn 的负向直接效应在 Q1/Q2/Q3 均显著，沙丁鱼丰度（Ns）在 Q2/Q4 负向显著；1 龄+鯖鱼丰度 0.8–14.2×10⁹ 尾（2007–2017），沙丁鱼 0.5–31.4×10⁹ 尾 [Kamimura et al. 2021](https://academic.oup.com/icesjms/article/78/9/3254/6380055 "ICES J. Mar. Sci. — Figure 7")。
- 西北太平洋 17 个种群 DFA 分析（1978–2018）：2010 年代出现同步体重下降，与鯖鱼+沙丁鱼+鳀鱼总生物量显著负相关（r=−0.84/−0.85/−0.74, 调整后 P<0.05）；气候驱动的上层海洋层化加剧放大了竞争效应——表层至 200m 温差显著升高 [Zhen & Ito 2024](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/faf.12818 "Fish and Fisheries")。

- 2024–2025 年日本鯖鱼渔获日益以小型低脂鱼为主，多家加工企业（如木の屋石巻水産、岩手県産）因采购不到足够大的鱼而停产；FY2023 渔获量约 271,000 t，较 5 年前近乎减半 [Asahi Shimbun](https://www.asahi.com/ajw/articles/16122566 "Poor catches cut canned mackerel production in half, 2025")。

## Chapter 4：Oceanographic Drivers — SST Anomalies, Kuroshio Variability, ENSO, and PDO Effects on Stock Distribution and Body Condition

### 研究目标
- How do SST anomalies, Kuroshio Current path variability (meander vs. straight path), ENSO phases (El Niño/La Niña), and PDO regimes affect (a) spatial distribution and migration, (b) recruitment success and cohort strength, and (c) growth rates and body condition of chub mackerel?
- What were the oceanographic conditions during the study period (April 2025–March 2026), and how do they compare to historical baselines?
- Synthesize published HSI models, temperature-recruitment relationships, and regime-shift analyses to provide the oceanographic context for biological and economic patterns.

### 关键发现
- 产卵适宜 SST 15–22°C，最优约 18°C；TSI 服从高斯分布（μ=18°C, σ=2.0°C）[Wang et al. 2022](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/marine-science/articles/10.3389/fmars.2022.996626/full "Frontiers in Marine Science 9:996626") [Takasuka et al. 2008](https://doi.org/10.3354/meps07407 "MEPS 360:211–217")。
- ARDL-ECM 模型显示 SSTA 对 0 龄、1–2 龄、≥3 龄三个年龄组的丰度均有显著正向影响（短期 P<0.05，长期 P<0.001），适度升温有利于产卵栖息地和早期存活 [Li et al. 2025](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1385110124000947 "J. Sea Research 203:102561")。
- TSI 体制转变：太平洋沿岸 NSTSI 于 1979/1980 年发生体制转变（与太平洋种群从 120 万吨崩溃至 2.8 万吨同步）；东海 ESTSI 和对马海峡 JSTSI 于 1990 年代末突变（与 TWC 种群衰退同步）；均由 Rodionov STARS 检测（L=10, H=1, 5%显著水平）[Wang et al. 2022](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/marine-science/articles/10.3389/fmars.2022.996626/full "Figures 3–4")。
- 东海夏季 HSI 模型（Chen et al. 2009）：>90% 渔获出现在 SST 28.0–29.4°C、SSS 33.6–34.2、Chl-a 0.15–0.50 mg/m³、SSHA −0.1–1.1m 的水域 [Chen et al. 2009](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s10872-009-0009-9 "J. Oceanography 65:93–102")。
- RCP8.5 ROMS 预测（2010s→2050s）：TWC 种群产卵场将北移，10m 水温升高 0.90–1.42°C；育幼场从日本海/东海向韩国海峡和黄海西移 [Go et al. 2025](https://www.mdpi.com/2410-3888/10/1/20 "Fishes 10(1):20")。
- 黑潮流量与鯖鱼丰度：冬季正相关、夏季负相关（强夏季黑潮将早期鯖鱼输送至不利环境）；长期显著影响 ≥3 龄鱼丰度 (P<0.001) [Li et al. 2025](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1385110124000947 "J. Sea Research 203:102561")。
- 亲潮面积显著影响 <2 龄鯖鱼丰度（短期 P<0.001，长期 P<0.05）；亲潮南限显著影响 0 龄（短期 P<0.001）和 ≥1 龄（长期 P<0.01）[Li et al. 2025](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1385110124000947 "J. Sea Research 203:102561")。
- 黑潮-亲潮过渡区（35–40°N，~10°C/500km 温度梯度）是加入量决定性区域；1988 年该区从冷态转暖态，伴随沙丁鱼崩溃（加入量从 213×10⁹ 降至 14×10⁹）和秋刀鱼/鳀鱼激增 [Watanabe 2009](https://journal.nafo.int/Volumes/Articles/ID/437/ "J. Northw. Atl. Fish. Sci. 41:197–204")。
- 最适生长温度假说：沙丁鱼 16°C、鯖鱼 18°C、鳀鱼/竹筴鱼 22°C——过渡区 SST 变化通过生长率选择性存活机制驱动多种小型中上层鱼类体制转换 [Takasuka et al. 2008, cited in Watanabe 2009](https://journal.nafo.int/Volumes/Articles/ID/437/ "NAFO J.")。
- 东海 HSI 模型（Yu et al. 2018）：适度厄尔尼诺事件扩大鯖鱼适宜栖息地（SSTA↑, SSHA↓, NPPA↓），但极端事件（如 2015/2016）效果复杂甚至负面 [Yu et al. 2018](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0165783618301772 "Fisheries Research 207:63–73")。
- SOI 对太平洋沿岸 NSTSI 有显著影响，建立了 ENSO→温度适宜性→加入量的直接路径；ENSO 被确认为 1970 年代末太平洋种群崩溃的外部驱动力 [Wang et al. 2022](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/marine-science/articles/10.3389/fmars.2022.996626/full "Discussion section")。
- 秘鲁近海：2023 厄尔尼诺期间鯖鱼北移至赤道表层水主导区域（SST 18–24°C, SSS 34.9–35.4）；2024 年后厄尔尼诺冷却期初级生产力回升、成鱼异常集中于南部 [Gutiérrez et al. 2024](https://www.sprfmo.int/assets/Meetings/02-SC/12th-SC-2024/Habitat-Monitoring/SC12-HM05-PER-SPRFMO-Habitat-for-Jack-and-chub-mackerel-Peru-2022-2024.pdf "SPRFMO SC12-HM05")。
- PDO 自 2020 年 1 月起持续负相位（2024 年 11 月 −3.13, 2026 年 2 月 −0.42），历史上负 PDO 与沙丁鱼减少/鯖鱼和鳀鱼可能有利条件相关 [NOAA NCEI PDO](https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/access/monitoring/pdo/ "PDO index through Feb 2026")。
- 经典 sardine-mackerel 体制转换假说（Chavez et al. 2003）：太平洋在"鳀鱼体制"（冷 PDO）与"沙丁鱼体制"（暖 PDO）间交替；但百年尺度相关性较弱，实际种群响应比简单 PDO 强迫更复杂 [Chavez et al. 2003](https://doi.org/10.1126/science.1075880 "Science 299:217–221")。
- 研究期（2025.4–2026.3）ENSO 状态：2023–2024 年初强厄尔尼诺（ONI 峰值+2.1）→ 2024 下半年快速转为中性/弱拉尼娜（ONI −0.1 至 −0.6）→ 2025–2026 年 2 月弱拉尼娜持续（ONI=−0.4）；2026 年 4 月预计转中性，6–8 月厄尔尼诺概率 62% [NOAA CPC ONI](https://www.cpc.ncep.noaa.gov/products/analysis_monitoring/ensostuff/ONI_v5.php "ONI data") [NOAA CPC ENSO Discussion](https://www.cpc.ncep.noaa.gov/products/analysis_monitoring/enso_advisory/ensodisc.shtml "Mar 2026")。
- 2024–2025 年全球 SST 尽管拉尼娜和负 PDO 仍异常偏暖；2025 年全球地表温度为 NOAA 176 年记录第三高（+1.17°C），全球均 SST 2025 年中达 20.89±0.06°C [NOAA NCEI](https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/news/global-climate-202513 "Global Climate Report 2025") [Mercator Ocean](https://www.mercator-ocean.eu/bulletin/ocean-temperature-bulletin-2025-mid-year-highlights/ "Ocean Temperature Bulletin 2025")。
- 所有 TSI-丰度关系均由非平稳 TGAM 优于平稳 GAM 拟合，表明海洋-生物关系存在阈值突变而非线性外推 [Wang et al. 2022](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/marine-science/articles/10.3389/fmars.2022.996626/full "Figure 6")。
- 梯度森林分析：EOF2（黑潮轴附近 TSI 变化，1–2 年滞后）和 JSTSI 是解释鯖鱼丰度变异的首要贡献者；PDO/SOI 等气候指数直接重要性可忽略——气候变异通过区域温度适宜性间接作用 [Wang et al. 2022](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/marine-science/articles/10.3389/fmars.2022.996626/full "Figure 5")。
- 2010 年代鯖鱼体重下降受上层海洋层化加剧放大：过渡区和亚北极区 VTD（表层-200m 温差）2007–2014 显著高于 1982–1989，抑制营养盐供给和初/次级生产力 [Zhen & Ito 2024](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/faf.12818 "Fish and Fisheries")。

## Chapter 6：Synthesis — Correlating Marine Environment, Resource Quality, Market Dynamics, and Fishery Economics

### 研究目标
- What are the demonstrable, evidence-based correlations linking oceanographic conditions → chub mackerel biological quality (body condition, size-at-age, stock abundance) → market supply and product quality → wholesale price formation and economic outcomes?
- Can a conceptual (or semi-quantitative) causal model be constructed tracing how an oceanographic regime shift or ENSO event propagates through the biological and economic system with identifiable time lags?
- What are the implications for fishery management, market forecasting, and climate adaptation in Pacific Rim mackerel fisheries?
- Forward outlook through September 2026 on how anticipated oceanographic conditions may affect resource quality and market dynamics.

### 关键发现
- 2018 年全球综述确认 35 个生态-经济渔业整合模型（IEEFM），但无一针对太平洋鯖鱼；经济子模型多为静态价格函数，无法捕捉小型中上层鱼类的供给驱动价格波动 [Nielsen et al. 2018](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1111/faf.12232 "Fish and Fisheries 19:1–29")。
- 西北太平洋鯖鱼尚无统一量化模型连接"气候→生物→经济"全链条；最接近的综合框架由三组独立研究拼合：(a) 气候→产卵栖息地 [Wang et al. 2022](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/marine-science/articles/10.3389/fmars.2022.996626/full)，(b) 环境+密度→生长/肥满度 [Kamimura et al. 2021](https://academic.oup.com/icesjms/article/78/9/3254/6380055)，(c) 渔获/规格→市场价格 [Promar/NSC 2018](https://www.seafood.no/globalassets/aktuelt/webinar/livemoter-presentasjoner/japan-mackerel-report-promar-2018.pdf)。
- Lehodey et al. (2006) 综述确认 ENSO/PDO 通过 SST、上升流、洋流输运和营养级生产力在多时间尺度驱动鱼类种群动态；1972/73 秘鲁鳀鱼崩溃（捕获量一年内降 60%）和 1997/98 鲣鱼价格暴跌 60% 为气候→市场冲击的经典案例 [Lehodey et al. 2006](https://journals.ametsoc.org/view/journals/clim/19/20/jcli3898.1.xml "J. Climate 19:5009–5030")。
- Free et al. (2019)：1930–2010 年海洋升温使全球 235 个鱼类种群 MSY 平均下降 4.1%；东海（鯖鱼核心渔场）为 MSY 下降区域之一 [Free et al. 2019](https://www.science.org/doi/10.1126/science.aau1758 "Science 363:979–983")。
- 因果链时滞估计：(1) ENSO/PDO→产卵栖息地 0–6 月；(2) 栖息地→加入量 1–2 年；(3) 加入量→密度依赖体况影响 2–5 年；(4) 渔获量/质量→批发价 3–12 月；全链（ENSO→市场价格）约 2–5 年（生物路径）+ 3–12 月（市场传导）[综合自 Wang et al. 2022, Kamimura et al. 2021, Watanabe & Yatsu 2004, Promar/NSC 2018]。
- 太平洋三文鱼为最接近的类比：PDO 体制转换后 2–3 年级出现渔获响应，随后因丰度增加产生密度依赖性体型下降——与鯖鱼模式完全平行 [Lehodey et al. 2006](https://journals.ametsoc.org/view/journals/clim/19/20/jcli3898.1.xml "J. Climate 19:5009–5030")。
- 体况→市场价值联系虽无直接 Kn-to-price 回归发表，但多条证据收敛：(a) 规格分级（400–600g 较 300–500g 溢价约 USD 200/MT）；(b) 脂肪含量决定价格层（挪威 26–30% vs 鯖鱼 13–20%，价差 2.8×）；(c) 丰洲市场拍卖师称 <500g 鯖鱼"无法以标准价出售"，加工商因无法采购大鱼而停产；(d) Kn 与脂肪含量强正相关 [Easyfish 2025](https://www.easyfish.net/en/frozen-atlantic-mackerel-supply-sourcing-guide-2025/ "Sourcing Guide") [Asahi Shimbun](https://www.asahi.com/ajw/articles/16122566 "2025") [Yatsu et al. 2019](https://www.jstage.jst.go.jp/article/jsfo/83/1/83_19/_article/-char/en "Bull. JSFO 83(1)")。
- 产地到批发倍数约 4.7–5.4×（2025 年产地 USD 1.96/kg vs 鲜品批发 USD 9.17–10.60/kg）；体况退化不仅线性压低单价，更将产品在价值层级间跳跃——从高端鲜品（>USD 10/kg）降级至出口/饲料（<USD 1/kg），10× 价值差。
- 2014/15 年加入量体制转变（2013 年级）：总生物量达历史高位 560 万吨，但密度依赖效应压低平均体重/肥满度/脂肪含量 → 记录高位生物量与飙升批发价并存的悖论——每单位质量（体型×脂肪）而非总量才是价格主导因素 [FRA Japan 2019](https://www.fra.go.jp/shigen/fisheries_resources/meeting/peer_review_meeting/files/2020/assess_masaba_p.pdf) [Kamimura et al. 2021](https://academic.oup.com/icesjms/article/78/9/3254/6380055)。
- FAO TP 667（2021）提供气候适应性渔业管理最全面框架，建议：将环境指标纳入收获控制规则、缩短评估周期、对体制转变风险设预防缓冲、实施动态空间管理；明确指出小型中上层鱼类为"最气候敏感物种" [FAO TP 667](https://openknowledge.fao.org/server/api/core/bitstreams/d15df88c-9c7c-4514-9ba9-5e6f22cd15a9/content "FAO, 2021")。
- Woods et al. (2022) 综述 1,801 条适应选项：渔业气候适应仍以生态韧性为主（配额限制、研究、执法），"市场多元化"和"价值链投资"仅居中等排位——经济/社会维度在适应规划中系统性不足 [Woods et al. 2022](https://academic.oup.com/icesjms/article/79/2/463/6354503 "ICES J. Mar. Sci. 79(2):463–479")。
- Yatsu et al. (2005) 直接建议将环境效应纳入日本鯖鱼管理，指出鯖鱼"可能部分因环境恢复有利而从过度捕捞状态恢复" [Yatsu et al. 2005](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1111/j.1365-2419.2005.00335.x "Fish. Oceanogr. 14:263–278")。
- NPFC 首次正式评估（2024–2025）和 CMM 2024-07（100,000 mt 限额）标志制度里程碑，但尚未纳入气候变量或环境关联收获控制规则 [NPFC CMM 2024-07](https://www.npfc.int/system/files/2025-03/NPFC-2025-COM09-WP04%20Rev.1%20CMM%202024-07%20for%20Chub%20Mackerel%20By%20Japan_0.pdf "Mar 2025")。
- ENSO 展望（NOAA CPC, 2026.3.30）：2026 年 4 月拉尼娜→中性转换；5–7 月中性（55%）；6–8 月厄尔尼诺可能出现（62%）。PDO 自 2020 年持续负相位（2025 年 7 月 −4.0σ 创 170 年记录最极端月值；2026 年 2 月 −0.42 有所缓和） [NOAA CPC](https://www.cpc.ncep.noaa.gov/products/analysis_monitoring/lanina/enso_evolution-status-fcsts-web.pdf "Mar 2026") [NOAA NCEI PDO](https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/access/monitoring/pdo/ "Through Feb 2026")。
- 生物展望：负 PDO 在 Chavez 框架下对鯖鱼/鳀鱼中性至有利；新兴厄尔尼诺可能改善产卵 TSI，但 Wang et al. (2022) 证实关系非平稳——无法从历史经验可靠预测当前 PDO-ENSO 组合效应。
- 市场展望（至 2026 年 9 月）：供给双重约束——NE Atlantic（挪威 2026 年配额再降 44%）+ NW Pacific（日本 FY2023 渔获减半至约 271,000 t，小鱼主导）。即使厄尔尼诺改善 NW Pacific 产卵条件，加入量效益需 2028–2029 年后才能进入渔获，2026 年 9 月前价格预计维持高位（日本鲜品批发 USD 10+/kg），无逆转信号。
- 综合因果链七步路径：(1) 气候强迫→区域 SST/栖息地（0–6 月）→ (2) 加入量（0–2 年）→ (3) 生物量/密度（1–3 年）→ (4) 体况/脂肪含量（同步，累计 2–5 年）→ (5) 产品质量层级（捕捞即时）→ (6) 批发/进口价（0–3 月）→ (7) 市场结构调整（数月至数年）。
